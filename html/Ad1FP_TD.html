Stop Thief!

RAN away from the subscriber in the beginning of September last, a negro man named Old JACK, aged about 53 years, five feet in height, and very same on account of a broken thigh. The laid Jack is very talkative, polite in his behaviour, and stole a bay mate when he absconded. The said runaway was seen in the neighborhood of Chillicothe. Ten Dollars reward will be given for taking him up, or securing him in any jail. Letters directed to Marietta Post Office, will be attended to.
