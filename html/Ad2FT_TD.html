Two hundred Dollars Reward.

Ran away from the subscriber, on the 9th inst. A Negro Man, named Bock; a bright mulatto, about 23 years of age, 5 feet 11 inches or 6 feet high, rather slender, straight made, has a down look when spoken to: as he had a variety of clothing it is difficult to describe them. He has been accustomed to work in a Tobacco manufactory, and is a good rough shoemaker. GEORGE ATKINSON.

Henderson, Ky. 18th
